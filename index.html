Hi, I am Shalin Shah

I am a multidisciplinary designer with an interest in emerging technology, sustainability & behavior change

I have had extensive experience in traditional field and design research in diverse contexts ranging from rural India to Urban Copenhagen. Currently, I am most interested in exploring the role of emerging technologies in developing new products and services, especially in the space of health (including mental health), sustainability, and mobility. In another life, I went to graduate school for Cognitive Science, and as such am quite interested in using decision-making and behavioral design.

In my free time, I enjoy learning how to code especially using p5.js, and HTML/CSS. Infact this website, is an exercise in hand-coding.
